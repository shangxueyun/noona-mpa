1.How to Get Rewards

During the event, you and your friends will be awarded 5 pesos. Up to 20 new people can be invited every day. The award will be distributed within 24 hours.

You will get a 20% commission from the daily mining rewards of your friends during their first 10 days on Noona! After 10 days, commissions will end, so keep inviting new friends!

2.What Users Can Be Invited?

Invited friends must be unregistered users of Noona. Friends who have been invited by others should not be invited repeatedly.

* Noona strictly prohibits all acts of cheating or breach of good faith. If cheating is found, we have the right to cancel the invitation award and investigate its legal liability.
